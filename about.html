ABOUT US

Who are we?

We are a team of students that work on reporting, going over, and announcing our school's sports.

To learn more about specific members or who our members are, visit our Leadership Pages.

Presidents --- Vice Presidents --- Current Leadership

What do we do?

We broadcast, make graphics, and report for the sports of Cox Mill High School.

Why do we do it?

We are able to improve student's broadcasting skills for the future, we help student sport-watchers get more involved and we make announcements or updates about our school's sports more accessible.

How do we do it?

We have a team of students that all are split up to work on different aspects of our Sports Network. Some of these jobs include broadcasting, graphic creation, interviewing, reporting, managing, announcements, and hopefully a future podcast.
